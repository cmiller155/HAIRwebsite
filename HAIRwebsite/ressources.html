Online was where I found myself mostly inspired. Essence is the magazine where I found all my hair products, hairstyles, beautiful black women, etc. Essence is all about focusing on reaching an audience of black women. The magazine covers fashion,lifestyle, and beauty tips for women of color.

Essence became my all time favorite magazines for hair tips because of all the beautiful versatility in different hair types, textures, and how to love your hair. They would include famous celebrties ranging from a young age and above. About how these girls and women rocked their curls no matter the type. Essence included hair produtcs and tricks that I have followed and go by.
